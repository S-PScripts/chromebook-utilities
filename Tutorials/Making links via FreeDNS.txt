Making links via FreeDNS:
1. Go to https://freedns.afraid.org/signup/?plan=starter
2. Fill in the details to sign-up, then go to your inbox to activate the account.
- You don't need to put your real name, and you can put in a temporary email address.
3. Make sure you are at https://freedns.afraid.org/subdomain/?ls=1
4. Click "Add a subdomain".
5. Do the following:
- Set Type to A.
- Enter anything as the subdomain.
- Choose any domain from the dropdown list.
- Set Destination to an IP address from BYOD (depending on what prxy you want to use)
6. Complete the captcha, then click "Save!".
7. Wait until the link propogates (this can take a few minutes). You can also create more domains if you want.

WARNING: FreeDNS will likely ban your account after a few days.
