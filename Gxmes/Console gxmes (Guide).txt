How to play console games:
1. Go to an unblocked game website (find them in the Emulator file in this repo!)
2. Find a game.
3. Download it.
4. Go to an online emulation website (find them in the Emulator file)
5. Find the console the game is run on or click auto-detect.
6. Click upload file.
7. Find the rom file and press done.
8. Play your game!

YOU CAN ALSO PLAY MANY THAT ARE BUILT INTO SOME OF THE WEBSITES IN THE HUBS.
